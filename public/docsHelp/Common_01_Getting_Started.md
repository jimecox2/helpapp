We offer monthly subscriptions for our products, with product technical support via email included in the pricing. For additional management consulting services, reach out on an ad-hoc basis by [contacting us](https://www.timebars.com/sales/contact-us).

Here’s how to subscribe:
1. **Register**: Visit the [registration page](https://www.timebars.com/auth/new-user) and sign up with an email and password.

4. **Log In**: Use your email/password to log into the app. The license downloads automatically, removing data limitations so you can keep working.

### How to Use the Spreadsheets!

#### Download the Spreadsheet
- [Libre Office Calc SS File](https://cdn.timebars.com/common/tbClientAllSheetsMasterDec30.ods)
- [MS Office Excel File](https://cdn.timebars.com/common/tbClient2023v1.xlsm.removeMe)

*Note*: The file name must begin with "tbClient" for the import to work.

The spreadsheets include seed data and demo projects. To get started:
1. Open the spreadsheet.
2. Copy and paste your task and project names over the rows in the Timebars tab.
3. Save the spreadsheet, then drag and drop it onto the Canvas. Your project data will load into the app.

- **Backup**: Importing via drag-and-drop creates a backup of existing data in your browser’s default folder (usually Downloads). Restore it by dragging the backup back onto the Canvas.
- **Post-Import**: After importing, log in again and refresh the browser to apply your license.
- **Data Validation**: Use the macro-based validator in the spreadsheet to check for dirty data (highlighted in red, e.g., missing mandatory columns).
- **Visualize Data**: Drop the file onto the Canvas to see your data. Export it back to the spreadsheet for bulk edits or analysis. Use the Portfolio tab (macros enabled) for custom reports.
- **Sync Cycle**: Repeat this sync for daily, weekly, or monthly needs. Spreadsheets eliminate clunky data entry forms, boosting productivity.


#### Personal Productivity
- Enter data in your favorite spreadsheet, then drag and drop it onto the Canvas for instant productivity.
- Work on one device, publish to the Timebars Cloud, then download and continue on another with one click after logging in.
- Publish multiple datasets (e.g., Sprints or Backlogs) and select one as active for Dashboard charts and reports.
- **Scheduling Flexibility**: Built-in rules automatically calculate time-phased results for charts, reports, and dashboards.
- **Save Money**: Replace expensive desktop or clunky cloud tools with our products to regain control of your data and time.
- Track tasks and resource forecasts easily—create projects manually or import via spreadsheet sync.
- Set unlimited project/task baselines for enhanced visualizations and progress reporting.
- Gain visibility into organizational work to prevent overload—simpler than MS Project and more modern.

#### High Security
- HTTPS/TLS end-to-end encryption, like banks use.
- Username and password sent securely over HTTP/TLS, never in the clear.
- No cookies stored on your device, reducing security risks.
- Password-encrypted tokens stored on our server, not your browser—unreadable by us or hackers.
